Offences & Penalties 

 

Power of RTO to detain the vehicle

1) Driving without effective driving License (sec 3,4 )  
2) Driving unregistered vehicle.(sec- 39 )  
3) Driving transport vehicle without permit or with breach of permit conditions attached to it.(sec- 66)  
4) Driving under the influence of drink or drugs.  
5) Rash and negligent driving.  
6) Non payment of taxes.(BMV Tax Act , 12B)
