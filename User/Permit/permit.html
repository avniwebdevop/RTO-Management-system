Permits 

 

Necessity of Permit ( Section 66 )

1 The owner of a transport vehicle cannot use his vehicle on any public place unless it is covered with a valid permit.
2 The transport vehicles of the central or state government , local authorities , ambulances , hearses ,  and those whose registered laden weight is not more than 3000 kgs. are exempted from permit.
3 All educational institutions buses require permit.
